Shree Jagannatha Temple Puri:


The Sun temple is situated in the small town of Konark situated in the Puri district of Odisha.



Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple:


Shree Padmanabhaswamy Temple is a Hindu temple, dedicated to Vishnu, in Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of the state ofKerala , India.



Shree Somnath Jyotirling Temple:


The Somanath temple or Deo Patan, is a Hindu temple located in Prabhas Patan, Veraval inGujarath, India.

Arulmigu Ramanathaswamy Temple:


Char Dham v t e Jagannath Rameswaram Dwarka Badrinath Ramanathaswamy Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva located on Rameswaram island in the state ofTamil Nadu, India.



Yamunotri temple:


Chota Char Dham Yamunotri Gangotri Kedarnath Badrinath Yamunotri Temple is situated in the western region of Garhwal Himalayas at an altitude of 3,291 metres in Uttarkashi district,Uttarakhand.



Lingaraj Temple:


Lingaraja Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva and is one of the oldest temples in Bhubaneswar, the capital of the Indian state of Odisha, India.
